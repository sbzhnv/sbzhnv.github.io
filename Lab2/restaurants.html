Restaurants

  1. Aroma

    2. A city cafe where it's nice to start the morning and end the day

    Abylay Khan Street in the segment from Kabanbay Batyr to Bogenbay Batyr is a place historically inhabited by coffee shops and known for its summer terraces. The Parisian bistro "Aroma", located directly opposite the Conservatory, became a symbol of this street in the mid-10s.














  2. Daredzhani

    3. A Georgian restaurant that will give you a new gastronomic experience

    "Daredzhani" reveals the fullness of traditional dishes, but also shows that Georgian cuisine is one of the most dynamically developing gastrocultures in the world. Drawing inspiration from classic recipes, they managed to create twists that allow you to fall in love with familiar combinations again.














  3. Luckee Yu

    4. The whole palette of modern Asian cuisine for residents of megacities

    Remember the characters and atmosphere of Wong Kar-Wai's films - food always occupies a special place in his paintings. No one else knew how to draw this universe of Hong Kong bistros, where people fall in love, dance, break up and dream. All this - along with a bottle of cold beer and hot nuddles.
